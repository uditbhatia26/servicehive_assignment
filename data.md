# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Overview
AutoStream is a SaaS platform providing automated video editing tools tailored specifically for content creators.

## Pricing & Subscription Plans

### Basic Plan
* **Price:** $29/month 
* **Video Limit:** 10 videos per month 
* **Resolution:** 720p resolution 

### Pro Plan
* **Price:** $79/month 
* **Video Limit:** Unlimited videos 
* **Resolution:** 4K resolution 
* **Features:** Includes AI-generated captions
* **Support:** 24/7 dedicated support

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
* AutoStream offers a limited refund window. No refunds are provided after 7 days of purchase.

### Customer Support
* General support is available for all users.
* **24/7 Priority Support** is an exclusive feature available only to subscribers on the **Pro Plan**.
